About Me

Hello! I'm Bill (孙源鸿 / 孫源鴻 / Yuan Hong Sun)—born in Wuhan, China and raised in Toronto, Canada. I'm an AI Engineer at Guidepoint, and previously a Machine Learning and AI Engineer at Kinaxis (supply chain ML and generative AI) and a Data Science Software Engineer at Nuralogix on the Anura health app.

I hold an M.S. in Computer Science from Georgia Tech, an M.A. in Applied Psychology from the University of Toronto, and a Bachelor's in Engineering Science from U of T, majoring in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence. I like building with software, ML, and data to tackle hard problems and improve people's lives.

Below you'll find selected projects and publications. I'm also active in extracurriculars and open to mentorship. You can take a look at my résumé. Please reach me by email or LinkedIn, and read occasional notes on my blog.
