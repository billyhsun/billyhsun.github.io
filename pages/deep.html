DEEP Summer Academy - Bill Sun

DEEP Summer Academy

University of Toronto


During the summer of 2015, I participated in University of Toronto's Da Vinci Engineering Enrichment Program (DEEP), a selective engineering summer camp for high school students. It was an eye-opening experience that ultimately led me to pursue engineering as a profession and enroll in University of Toronto's Engineering Science Program.

During the 5 weeks there, I took part in the following programs:

1. Designing and Building a Computer

2. Advanced Manufacturing: 3D printing

3. Magnetic Materials

4. Autonomous Vehicles Design

5. Engineering Leadership and Innovation
